## II. The Rules-Based Order as Accumulated Influence

## VII. The End of the Unipolar Moment

What remains is unipolar inertia.

Institutions persist.

Late-stage unipolarity eliminates automatic alignment.

## VIII. Multipolar Transition

Multipolar transition does not require parity.
It requires autonomy.

When multiple actors can:

* control critical technological nodes,
* route around institutional chokepoints,

The unipolar moment ends not when dominance disappears,
but when influence no longer structurally constrains alternatives.

Multipolar transition begins when optionality scales.

This is not ideological transformation.
It is redistribution of leverage.
